Our Services

Comprehensive forensic behavioural and intelligence analysis services designed to provide legally defensible insights for complex cases and investigations.

Forensic Document Review

Expert analysis of documents for authenticity, alterations, and content integrity. Includes handwriting analysis, signature verification, and examination of production methods.

  • Uncover hidden truths
  • Validate evidence
  • Strengthen legal cases
  • Ensure document integrity

Audio Analysis & Transcription

Advanced analysis including voice identification, transcription of degraded recordings, and authentication of audio files. Enhancement and extraction of critical information for court-ready reports.

  • Identify speakers
  • Clarify conversations
  • Authenticate recordings
  • Accurate transcripts

Financial Review & Fraud Detection

In-depth analysis to detect fraud, trace assets, and identify illicit activities. Transaction tracing, pattern identification, and detailed reporting for civil or criminal matters.

  • Trace hidden assets
  • Support investigations
  • Ensure compliance

Psychological and behavioural profiles to understand motives, predict behaviour, and assess risk. Useful for investigations and corporate security contexts.
