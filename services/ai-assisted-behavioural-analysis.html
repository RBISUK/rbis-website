AI-Assisted Behavioural Analysis

RBIS combines proprietary machine learning models with human expertise to interpret tone, intent, and behavioural signals across large datasets. Automated pattern recognition accelerates investigations while analysts maintain contextual judgement.

Use Cases

  • Screening large volumes of communication for misconduct indicators.
  • Mapping timelines of disputes to visualise escalation points.
  • Highlighting anomalies in resident or staff narratives for further review.

Compliance Notes

Outputs are advisory and require human validation before any decision is made. Models are trained on anonymised data and operate under documented UK GDPR legitimate-interest assessments.
