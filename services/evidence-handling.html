Evidence Handling & Verification

RBIS receives and processes digital and physical evidence using tamper-evident channels and forensic workflows. Analysts document each step to preserve provenance, ensuring that intake, storage, and export meet evidential standards.

Use Cases

  • Securely capturing resident complaints and supporting media.
  • Validating third‑party disclosures before legal escalation.
  • Normalising disparate data sources for investigatory reports.

Compliance Notes

All submissions follow UK GDPR consent and minimisation principles. Chain‑of‑custody logs, hashing, and controlled retention policies enable courtroom admissibility and client audits.
