OmniAssist Platform

OmniAssist delivers configurable workflows that span repairs, compliance, and customer service. Modules plug together via a common data layer, enabling rapid deployment of new processes without bespoke development.

Use Cases

  • Housing providers unifying disparate case management tools.
  • Compliance teams orchestrating evidence collection across departments.
  • Commercial operators standardising service delivery at scale.

Compliance Notes

Role‑based access controls, audit logging, and encryption are enabled by default. Tenant‑specific configuration allows controllers to implement their own retention schedules and privacy notices.
