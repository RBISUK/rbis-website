PACT Ledger

PACT Ledger converts organisational promises into immutable, evidence‑backed entries. Every commitment is timestamped, assigned ownership, and linked to supporting documents, creating a single source of truth for accountability.

Use Cases

  • Tracking remedial actions from ombudsman or regulator findings.
  • Managing service‑level agreements across complex supply chains.
  • Demonstrating fulfilment of corporate pledges or board directives.

Compliance Notes

The ledger maintains immutable audit trails while respecting subject rights through export and erasure workflows. Data is compartmentalised by project and secured with encryption at rest and in transit.
