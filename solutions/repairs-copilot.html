Repairs & Compliance Copilot

The Copilot platform tracks repair obligations and statutory inspections in real time. It automates resident communications, schedules contractors, and generates evidence packs that withstand regulatory scrutiny.

Use Cases

  • Social landlords monitoring disrepair claims and response times.
  • Facilities teams coordinating gas, fire, and electrical safety checks.
  • Local authorities evidencing compliance against housing standards.

Compliance Notes

All tenant data is processed under controller instructions with role‑based access controls and audit logs. Configurable retention rules and exportable records support UK Housing Ombudsman and GDPR requirements.
